J. Paul Reed

Build/Release Engineering, DevOps, and Agile Consultant

J. Paul Reed, aka The Sober Build Engineer, has over a decade of experience in the trenches as a build/release and tools engineer, working with such organizations as VMware, Mozilla, Postbox, and Symantec. In 2012, he founded Release Engineering Approaches, a consultancy incorporating a host of tools and techniques to help organizations "Simply Ship. Every time." He's worked across a number of industries, from financial services to cloud-based infrastructure, with teams from 2 to 200. In 2014, he became a Visiting Scientist/Chief Delivery Officer at Praxisflow, Phoenix Project author Kevin Behr's firm. Paul is also the founding host of The Ship Show, a twice-monthly podcast tackling topics related to build engineering, DevOps, and release management.
