Why sponsor?

DevOpsDays is an excellent opportunity for vendors to interact with participants as well as engage in disucssions surrounding "best practices" of DevOps. Most in attendance are professionals in their field and early adopters of new methods and tools to provide software quickly and effeciently.

Gold and Silver sponsorship packages allow for table space for vendors to give demos, hold give-aways, and hold conversations with participants.

DevOpsDays does not provide a method for vendors to scan and track leads. Generally, vendors will manage their own methods of making contacts for future correspondance.

We love our sponsors and they are integral to putting on an amazing event such as DevOpsDays. However, sponsoring the event does not guarantee a speaking slot. Vendors are encouraged to submit talks during the "Call For Papers" period, but all abstracts will be considered equally.

DevOpsDays brings the best and brightest talent together to openly discuss and learn about DevOps "best practices". This event is an excellent opportunity for organizations seeking talented individuals to join your organization or project.
