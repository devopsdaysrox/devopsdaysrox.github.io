The Delphix DaaS (Data as a Service) software platform brings the benefits of virtualization to application data. Specifically, Delphix captures and stores a copy of production application data, including ongoing changes. From this single data footprint, Delphix delivers virtual copies for application development, reporting, troubleshooting, or other purposes, without any data duplication or movement. As a result, application projects move faster, with higher quality, and at a lower cost.
